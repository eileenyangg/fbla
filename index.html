OUR FEATURES

FAMILY BONDING

Here at The Leisure Club, we strongly cherish the gift of family. Spend a relaxing day in our club spas while bonding with the family.

PHOTOGRAPHERS

We offer complimentary photos during our exclusive parties, game nights, and recreational events. Professionally captured pictures will be emailed to you for free.

HOLIDAY SPECIALS

Visit The Leisure Club with the family on Holidays to receive exclusive deals on food, arcade tokens, and parties. We give out complimentary gift baskets during the week of Christmas.

CHILDREN CENTER

Have a child under eight? We have professionally experienced nannies and caretakers who run our club's Child Entertainment Center. Drop off your child for only $10 an hour while you relax.

SNACK BAR

Our gourmet snack bar contains nutritious meals as well as snacks and a variety of drinks. Children under 10 get half off on weekdays.

GAME NIGHT

Every Friday Night is Game Night! Families will receive complimentary beverages and light snacks. Games vary every week.

ABOUT THE LEISURE CLUB

Welcome to Our Website!

NEWLY OPENED FAMILY ENTERTAINMENT CENTER

  • RECREATION

At The Leisure Club, we offer recreational activities perfect for every member of the family. By signing up for a membership, you will be granted unlimited access to our olympic size outdoor swimming pool, arcade room, mini-golf course, fitness and yoga gym, and spa.

Sign up for a membership today by contacting us at the contact information located below.

Every Friday Night there is Family Game Night at the Leisure Club from 7:00pm to 10:00pm. Games vary every week, unlimited complimentary drinks are passed out throughout the night.

Our staff also plans bi-weekly Family Fun Events. Our entertainment center is conveniently located by the infamous Huntington Beach in Orange Country. Come join us Sunday evenings for a fun filled beach barbeque party. Families and friends of membership holders are able to come for free.

Several of our club staff are professional event planners. Have your birthday, graduation, work, or holiday party here at The Leisure Club. Our party specialists will help you create a throughly though out party plan down to every tiny detail.

Party bookings, availabilites, and important details are located at the bottom of the page.
